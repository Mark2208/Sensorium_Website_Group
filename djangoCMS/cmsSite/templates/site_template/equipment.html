EQUIPMENT

High Tech Equipment

Pictures and information about all our equipment.

Wearable eye tracking

Tobii Glasses 2 shows exactly what a person is looking at in real time, as he or she moves freely in any real-world environment or whilst using mobile devices insitu.
The eye tracking glasses help broaden insights into human behaviour across many research disciplines. 

Tobii Pro 2 Glasses 2 Wireless

Wearable eye tracking, see what the user sees in real time and know where they’re looking, 120 times a
second. Fully wireless and lightweight for use anywhere. 

Facial expression recogition

The Affectiva engine, called AFFDEX, identifies the face and then locates the
main feature points on the face, such as eyes and mouth. Then, it assesses
movement, shape and texture of the face.

AFFDEX

Can detect 7 basic emotions, valence, emotion channels, 33 facial landmarkds, head orientation, interocular distance and emotional engagement

TOBII X2-30

The Pro X2-30 is designed to capture data at 30 Hz in order to show exactly where people are
looking— the gaze point — rather than for research into the timing and duration of fixations. It can be fixed directly onto screens up to 24”, or used on larger screens by being mounted on a tripod. 

TOBII X2-30

Aggregated Heatmap, automatically sync all participant data or separate based on participant demographics such as age, gender or any variable.

GSR (Galvanic Skin Response)

The Shimmer GSR (Galvanic Skin Response) monitors skin conductivity between two reusable electrodes attached to two fingers of one hand. 

Shimmer GSR

Caused by a stimulus the sweat glands become more active, increasing moisture on the skin and allowing the current to flow more readily by changing the balance of positive and negative ions in the secreted fluid
(increasing skin conductance). 

EEG (Electroencepharlo
graphgy)

Detect emotional and motivational processes, use EEG to assess the emotional and cognitive responses, such as changes in emotional arousal and cognitive load.

B-Alert AT10

The B-Alert AT10 is the premiere solution for mobile EEG applications, delivering a balance of research grade robustness, functionality and simplicity. The 9-channel combination of mid-line and lateral EEG sites allows for multiple analysis opportunities to meet the needs of researchers across many fields.
